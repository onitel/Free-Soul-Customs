Lifestyle

We ride custom remade bikes ourselves all year round and we promise to treat your old buddy with the love, care and attention we would our own bike.

About us

Free Soul is a Romanian Brasov-based custom garage founded by Dorel Nemuritorul in 2021. In the workshop are conceived customized and hand finished motorcycles to create unique, one-of-a-kind machines. Our love of old timers is no secret, so every project we undertake honours a legend while telling it's own story.

Short about our services

We are proud to offer custom motorcycle builds, service and repair. Whether you want to build your dream bike from the ground up, or add mods and accessories, or just need to breathe new life into your machine, we are here to help get you back on the road in style.



Custom Bikes

As you can see, you can totally trust us to bring your old buddy, and we’ll put a new character-changing twist on its look. Still don’t own a bike? Just come by, or contact us and tell us your secret dream, and we’ll do our best to make it come true: from finding the donor bike and brainstorming it's proper custom design concept to turning the concept into reality.
